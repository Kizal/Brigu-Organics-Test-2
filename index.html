About us

Our Story

Bhrigu Organics, established in 2019, is a trading platform of Indian spices, food grains and other daily essentials. Our catalogue involves a variety of millets, grains, spices and jaggery products, all of which are organically prepared. To make sure that our customers get the best quality products, we constantly try to find innovative and natural methods of producing these goods. Procuring the produce directly from farmers and suppliers, all the products undergo rigorous rounds of quality checks and cleanliness. Only the chosen best products are supplied to your home. These guidelines have helped us maintain a high quality.

Why us?

Being brought in a middle class family we understand the dilemma of choosing between eating healthy and maintaining budget. So we at Bhrigu Organics work at are very best to provide you with the best of the products at reasonable prices along with providing farmers with their fare share.
